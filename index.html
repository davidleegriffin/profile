More About Me

I bring a knowledge of software engineering principles and concepts that I believe will be valuable to the you. I recently honed my abilities in software engineering by attending AppAcademy where I completed various projects to demonstrate ability in full-stack engineering, providing a solid foundation for the position of interest. My decision-making strength, people-centric nature and eagerness to please has yielded me excellent engineering skills. Those skills include: React, Redux, Express, Postgres, Python, JavaScript, and others too numerous to mention. Recently I completed an app called Daily Bread Delivery where I set up a backend authentication and database access using sequelize and Express interacting with a React frontend to prevent CORS vulnerabilities. I also completed an E-commerce shopping cart system using Redux and React to guide users with selection, pricing, and ordering products. And finally, I utilized React-slick to create a vibrant and dynamic carousel for products, enhancing overall user experience.

I am excited to contribute my strengths, experience, and leadership toward your team's efforts.
